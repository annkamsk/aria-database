Pour les couvents, cest fini, Marcel's aria from Les Huguenots

Pour les couvents, cest fini!	Its all up with the monasteries!
Les moines à terre,		Down with the monks,
guerre à tout cagot béni!	war upon all blessed canting hypocrites!
Papistes, la guerre!		War on Papists!
Livrons à la flamme, au fer	Well give over their temples of hell
leurs temples denfer,		to the fire and the sword,
Terrassons-les, cernons-les,	Throw them down, beleaguer them,
frappons-les, perçons-les!	strike them, run em through!
Piff, paff, piff, cernons-les!	Piff, paff, piff, lets run em through!
Piff, paff, piff, frappons-les!	Piff, paff, piff, strike em down!
Piff, paff, piff, paff!		Piff, paff, piff, paff!
Quils pleurent,		Let em cry,
quils meurent;			let em die;
mais grâce jamais!		but mercy never!

Jamais mon bras ne trembla	My arm never faltered
aux plaintes des femmes!	at the cries of women!
Malheur à ces Dalila		Woe betide those Delilahs
qui perdent les âmes!		who damn mens souls!
Brisons au tranchant du fer	Well destroy their hellish charms
leurs charmes denfer!		with the blade of the sword!
Ces beaux démons, chassez-les,	Drive these beautiful devils away,
traquez-les, frappez-les!	track em down, strike em down!
Piff, paff, pouff, chassez-les!	Piff, paff, pouff, hunt em!
Piff, paff, pouff, traquez-les!	Piff, paff, pouff, track em down!
Piff, paff, piff, paff!		Piff, paff, piff, paff!
Quils pleurent,		Let em cry,
quils meurent;			let em die;
mais grâce jamais!		but mercy never!
